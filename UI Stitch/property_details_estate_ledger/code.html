The Curation Notes

A masterclass in contemporary design, The Glass Pavilion offers an unparalleled living experience suspended between ocean views and canyon serenity. Designed by renowned architect Silas Vance, this estate seamlessly blurs the line between indoor and outdoor environments through monumental expanses of structurally glazed walls.

The main living gallery features 14-foot ceilings, bespoke European oak flooring, and a centerpiece dual-sided fireplace clad in imported Nero Marquina marble. The kitchen is an epicurean dream, outfitted with dual islands, seamlessly integrated Miele appliances, and custom walnut cabinetry that hides the robust functionality of a professional prep space.

Retreat to the primary suite, which cantilevers over the negative-edge pool, offering sweeping vistas of the Pacific coastline. The en-suite is a sanctuary of calm, featuring a monolithic soaking tub carved from a single block of stone, dual vanity stations, and a massive rain shower overlooking a private bamboo courtyard.
